Charging Schedule

SmartCHARGE is different from standard EV chargers because it allows you to set a charging schedule. Take advantage of Time of Use electricity pricing by setting a schedule in order to save on your electricity bill. To set or remove a charging schedule, navigate to the Configuration Page .
